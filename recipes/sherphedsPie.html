Vasili's Sheperd's Pie

This is a picture of my Sheperd's Pie

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • ground beef
  • Cream of mushroom

Steps

  1. cook ground beef
  2. add cream of mushroom
  3. Dice and boil potatos
  4. Transfer ground beef into large oven safe pan
  5. Mash boiled potatoes and spread over ground beef
  6. Set oven to 400oC
